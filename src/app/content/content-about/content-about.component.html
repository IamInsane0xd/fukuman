About the game

Fukuman is a quiz game where 2 teams race against each other. They answer trivia questions, and if their answer is correct, they can open one of 20 safes, two of which have bombs in them, the other ones having money ranging from 1,000 to 7,000,000 HUF.

The first game

Which team starts?

Firstly there is a guessing question, where the team that guessed closer will start the game.

The game

The game consists of 10 rounds. In each round the teams get a two choice question. First they only see the first option, and they get to decide if it’s true or false. If they chose false, they have no choice but to accept the second option, which they haven’t seen before. If a team managed to answer correctly on their turn, they get to open a safe. If the answer is wrong, it’s the next team’s turn. When opening a safe, the players can choose two safes, one of which will open. They can chose to keep it, or risk it and get the other safe’s contents which they haven’t seen yet. If the safe contains a bomb, it’s the other team’s turn. On the last round, the team currently on their turn has the chance to get into the finals. If they answer correctly, they move into the finals, if not, the other team moves on.

The finals

The finals consists of 5 questions, each of them are two choice. The players won’t know if they answered correctly or not. After the questions, the players are provided with 6 safes, one of which has their prize. They are then provided with the questions again with their answers, with the correct answers being the first ones. They then get to keep the question, or stop. If they keep the question, and the answer is correct, one safe that doesn’t have the price disappears. If they answer is incorrect, the game is over. If they chose to stop, they can guess which safe contains their price. If they chose correctly, they get to take the price home. If they don’t, they walk home empty handed.
